= Admin: Issue Certificates

In this section you will act as a training administrator — creating a course, enrolling a student, and issuing a blockchain certificate.

== Step 1: Log in to a Course Manager

. Open the *TechPulse Course Manager*: {techpulse_course_manager_url}
. Click *Login* and use the Keycloak credentials:
+
[cols="1,1"]
|===
| Username | `admin`
| Password | `admin`
|===

== Step 2: Create a Course

. Click *Create Course*.
. Fill in the details:
+
[cols="1,2"]
|===
| Field | Value

| Course Name | `OpenShift Administration`
| Description | `Red Hat OpenShift cluster administration fundamentals`
| Duration | `40`
|===
. Click *Save*.

== Step 3: Enroll a Student

. Select the course you just created.
. Click *Enroll Student*.
. Enter student details:
+
[cols="1,2"]
|===
| Field | Value

| Name | `Jane Doe`
| Email | `jane.doe@example.com`
|===
. Click *Enroll*.

== Step 4: Issue a Certificate

. In the enrolled students list, select *Jane Doe*.
. Click *Issue Certificate*.
. The certificate is now recorded on the Hyperledger Fabric blockchain.
. Copy the *Certificate ID* — you will need it in the next section.

[NOTE]
====
The certificate issuance calls the `cert-admin-api`, which submits a transaction to the `certcontract` chaincode. The transaction is endorsed by the peer, ordered by the BFT orderers, and committed to the ledger.
====
