= How to Use This Guide

Welcome to the *CertChain Blockchain Credentialing* demo on Red Hat OpenShift.

This lab guide walks you through a complete blockchain credentialing scenario — from issuing tamper-proof academic certificates on Hyperledger Fabric to verifying them through a public portal, and exploring the platform management capabilities underneath.
